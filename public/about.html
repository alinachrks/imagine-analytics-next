About Us

We are the world's largest company

Atmosfar has become a professional aluminium extrusion manufacturer for architectural systems and industrial applications.

Our factory is well equipped with 40 extrusion lines with a capacity of 500- 5500 tons. With a total annual output of 15000 tons.

Atmosfar produces a wide range of aluminum extruded products, including window and door systems, curtain walls, performance louver, heat sink, solar panel framing, etc. Customized service is available.

Our engineers can help you to design your own extrusion structure and produce your own system.
